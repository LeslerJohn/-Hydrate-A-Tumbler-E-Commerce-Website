Privacy Policy

edit

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we may collect, how we utilize this information, and circumstances under which we may disclose it. Additionally, it details the measures we've implemented to safeguard your personal information. By visiting our Site, you acknowledge and agree to adhere to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.


We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of any websites not affiliated with Hydrate.com that either link to or are linked from our Site.


INFORMATION COLLECTION


Hydrate may collect personal information about you. Examples of such information include your name, shipping and billing addresses, phone number, email address, credit card information, and customer preferences data. Methods through which we may collect such information include:

A. Before accessing certain sections of our Site or making purchases, you'll need to complete a registration form. This form will prompt you to provide various personal details such as your name, addresses, phone number, email address, and credit card number. Additionally, you may be asked to disclose your country of residence and/or your organization’s country of operation. These details are used for billing, order fulfillment, communication, and internal marketing purposes.

B. Certain areas of our Site may allow you to input your email address for purposes such as registering for our newsletter, blog, or creating a Hydrate account.

C. Similar to many other websites, we utilize cookie technology throughout our Site. Cookies are small files stored by your web browser on your device. They serve various purposes including enhancing security, personalizing content, and tracking orders and shopping cart items. Cookies also facilitate the sending of cart reminder emails. However, disabling cookies in your browser settings may limit your ability to shop on our Site. Nevertheless, customers who disable cookies can still place orders by contacting our customer service.

D. Hydrate may collect your email address through cookies and pixels on our Site, leveraging trusted third-party partners. These partners may merge your email information with other data they possess, such as mailing addresses, to enable us to deliver relevant marketing offers via direct mail. If you prefer not to have Hydrate collect information about you, please contact us to opt out.


INFORMATION USE


We do not sell or rent customer information, and all information provided to Hydrate remains confidential, except in the following instances:

A. We may share summarized or aggregated data about our customers with partners, advertisers, or other third parties. This information will not personally identify you.

B. We may share information with companies that provide support services to us, such as printers, mailing houses, fulfillment companies, or web hosts, to facilitate their functions.

C. Your personal information will be used to communicate with you about your orders, deliveries, and to deliver marketing or promotional materials if you opt-in. You can opt-out of such communications at any time.

D. We may be obligated to disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or requests from law enforcement officials, or if required by law.

E. We may publicly disclose the name, city, and state of the winner of any online sweepstakes or marketing campaigns.


PROTECTING YOUR INFORMATION

We have implemented reasonable measures to protect your personal information and to ensure the security of your orders and credit card information. Our servers use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) / Transport Layer Security (TLS) encryption, an advanced technology.

SSL/TLS is considered the industry standard and is among the leading protocol available to encrypt online transactions. Your personal information including your name, credit card number, and address is encrypted so that it cannot be read as it is transmitted over the Internet. Regardless of these efforts, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, any information you transmit to us is sent at your own risk.

Safe Shopping Guarantee

Under the Fair Credit Billing Act, your bank cannot hold you liable for more than $50.00 of fraudulent credit card charges. If your bank does hold you liable for any amount up to the full $50.00, Hydrate will cover the entire liability. If you suspect unauthorized use of your credit card, you must notify your credit card issuer in accordance with their reporting rules and procedures. Hydrate will cover this liability only if the unauthorized use of your credit card resulted from purchases made on our secure server through no fault of your own.

Children’s Privacy

We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly remove it from our records.

Opt Out/Corrections

To request corrections to your personal data or opt-out of communications, please contact us. Upon your request, we will correct or update your personal information, cease sending emails to your email address, and/or disable your account to prevent future purchases through it. Please do not include sensitive information, such as credit card numbers, in your email.

Offline Collection, Use & Disclosure of Information

While this Privacy Policy primarily applies to online collection of personal information, we also endeavor to protect the privacy of your data offline. For example, if you call us to place an order or ask questions, we will only request the necessary personal information to fulfill your request. Any information stored offline, such as order details, will be entered into our database using SSL/TLS encryption.

Policy Changes

We reserve the right to amend our Privacy Policy periodically. We encourage you to refer to this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed of any updates.

Questions & Feedback

We value your questions, comments, and concerns about privacy. Please feel free to contact us with any feedback or inquiries regarding privacy or any other issues at ConsumerSupport@cs.hydrate.com

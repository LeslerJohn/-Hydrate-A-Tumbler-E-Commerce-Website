7. Why is there condensation in my flask?

Condensation usually happens on the upper part where the cap/lid is located. This is normal because the cap is made of thermoplastic material. As far as product development goes, the double wall insulation technology can only be done to the actual tumbler that holds the liquid. Rest assure, this will not affect the temperature inside the flask. If condensation occurs on the actual flask, do let us know and we will assist you on this matter.

8. Why does my cold/hot water not last long for 24/12 hours?

When there is no moisture or condensation on the actual flask but the liquid does not last retain its cold or hot temperature, these are factors to consider:

a. Temperature of liquid poured into the flask.

b. How often the flask lid is removed/opened.
